Introduction

Georgia, located at the crossroads of Western Asia and Eastern Europe, has a rich and diverse history dating back to ancient times.

Early History

Georgia's early history is characterized by the development of early human settlements and the establishment of powerful kingdoms.

Modern History

In modern times, Georgia has experienced significant political and social changes, including independence from the Soviet Union.
